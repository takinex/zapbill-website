Frequently Asked Questions

If you can’t find what you’re looking for, contact us.

Does Zapbill support tax?

Yes, you can add taxes as inclusive or exclusive and specify the percentage.

How can I transfer data to a new device?

Backup your data to Google Drive, sign in on the new device with the same email, and restore your data.

Does the app work offline?

Yes, data is saved locally, so the app works without an internet connection.

Can I print invoices directly from the app?

Yes, connect a compatible Bluetooth or USB printer, then tap the Print icon on the invoice screen.

What printer paper sizes are supported?

The app supports 58mm, 80mm, and A4-sized printer paper.

Is a free trial available?

Yes, a 3-day free trial is available.

Can I use the app for free?

Yes, the app can be used for free with some limitations.

Is it secure to store my data?

Yes, all data is stored locally on your device, keeping it safe and private.

Can I track inventory?

Yes, you can track inventory and receive low stock alerts.

Is the app optimized for 10-inch tablets?

Yes, the app is fully optimized for 10-inch tablets.

Is a physical barcode scanner supported?

Yes, you can pair a compatible scanner with the app and start scanning immediately.
